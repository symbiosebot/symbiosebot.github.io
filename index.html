Frequently asked questions

Is Symbiose free?

Yes. The bot is free to use. If we ever add paid features, the core matching will remain free.

Do I need a Telegram account?

Yes — Symbiose runs through Telegram for speed, privacy and reliability.

How are matches found?

We search by opposite intent (learn/teach), skill relevance (exact or partial) and city. You confirm before contacts are shared.

What about privacy?

We only store what’s necessary for matching. You control when to share contact info.
